Recruitment Services FAQ

Get answers to the most common questions about our targeted ICT recruitment services

Do you offer recruitment services for ICT roles?

Yes - we provide targeted recruitment support for ICT roles. Whether you need a graduate, early-career professional, or a specialised tech candidate, we manage sourcing, screening, shortlisting, and interview coordination so you can focus on running your team.

What types of ICT positions can you recruit for?

We cover the full ICT spectrum, including:

  • Software, data, cybersecurity, cloud, networking, analytics
  • Business analysis, systems roles, support and service desk
  • SAP, Microsoft, Salesforce, and other platform specialists
  • Project coordinators, testers, QA, and digital roles

How does your recruitment process work?

We run a streamlined, no-nonsense process:

  1. Scoping session to understand your needs, tech stack, project demands, and ideal profile
  2. Active sourcing across our graduate pool, networks, and broader market
  3. Screening & behavioural interviewing to ensure technical and culture fit
  4. Shortlist presentation with only high-quality candidates
  5. Interview scheduling & coordination
  6. Reference checks & offer support

What makes your recruitment service different?

We specialise in early-career and tech talent - the group most employers struggle to hire well. We know what "great talent" looks like in this space, and we assess for attitude, passion, communication, and potential, not just keywords on a CV.

Do you only place graduates?

No. While graduates are our specialty, we also support junior and mid-level ICT recruitment depending on your needs. If you're looking for someone who's hungry, capable, and ready to grow - we'll find them.

How long does the recruitment process take?

It depends on the role, but most early-career placements take between 1-3 weeks from brief to shortlist. Hard-to-fill roles may take a little longer - but we move quickly and keep you updated at every stage.

Is there a fee to engage you for recruitment?

Yes - recruitment is a paid service and pricing depends on the role, seniority, and level of sourcing required. We'll give you a clear, upfront quote once we understand your needs.

Do you offer replacement guarantees?

Yes. We stand behind the quality of our candidates, and a replacement period is included in our recruitment service. If something's not the right fit, we'll work with you to make it right.

How do I get started with recruitment services?

Reach out through our contact form or book a call. Tell us what role you're struggling to fill - we'll take it from there.
