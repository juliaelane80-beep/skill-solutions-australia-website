Our Mission

To bridge the gap between ICT education and industry employment by providing comprehensive career services and meaningful internship opportunities that prepare graduates for successful, sustainable careers in Tasmania's growing technology sector.

Our Vision

A Tasmania where every ICT graduate has the skills, confidence, and opportunities to build a meaningful career, contributing to a thriving, innovative, and globally competitive technology ecosystem.

Our Core Values

Excellence

Meet Our Team

Leadership driving Tasmania's ICT future

Julia Lane

Director

With a decade of experience in the education and ICT sectors, I am passionate about empowering the next generation of professionals and connecting them with meaningful career opportunities. As the previous State Operations Manager at the Australian College of Applied Professions, I lead the Professional Year Program, managing operations across multiple States and ensuring that each graduate is equipped with a clear career development path.

My expertise lies in designing and implementing comprehensive Future Ready Programs that address the skills gap between recent graduates and industry needs. Through tailored programs and strategic internships, I help graduates gain hands-on experience, develop essential professional skills, and build strong industry connections. I have a proven track record in recruitment and talent management, leveraging platforms like LinkedIn to attract and engage top talent.

My leadership style is respectful, inclusive, and dedicated to fostering growth and success within my teams and among the graduates I support. As a forward-thinking professional, I am committed to bridging the divide between academia and industry, driving performance, and supporting Tasmania's evolving tech landscape.

My mission is to enhance workforce readiness, contribute to economic growth, and create pathways for talented individuals to thrive in their chosen careers. Feel free to connect with me to explore opportunities for collaboration or to discuss how we can work together to shape the future of the ICT sector in Tasmania.

Frequently Asked Questions

Get to know what we do, who we support, and how we can help you succeed

What does Skills Solutions Australia do?

We connect talented ICT and engineering graduates with real industry experience through structured internships, job-ready coaching, and short-term contract opportunities. Our mission is simple: help graduates step confidently into the workforce while giving employers access to emerging talent.

Who are your programs designed for?

Our programs support:

  • ICT graduates looking to start their tech career
  • Tasmanian employers looking for extra capability on projects and employees

If you're building or supporting tech in Tasmania - you belong here.

Who do we support?

We work with graduates across the full spectrum of ICT and technology disciplines, including:

Software Development & Engineering

Front-end, back-end, full-stack, mobile apps, web development, DevOps, automation, and QA/testing.

Cybersecurity

SOC analysts, security operations, threat intelligence, GRC, compliance, vulnerability assessment, incident response, and cloud security fundamentals.

Data & Analytics

Data analysis, data engineering, business intelligence, machine learning fundamentals, database management, SQL, Power BI, and reporting.

Cloud & Infrastructure

AWS, Azure, Google Cloud, networking, systems administration, technical support, virtualisation, IT operations, endpoint management, and service desk roles.

Business & Systems

Business analysis, systems analysis, requirements gathering, process mapping, digital transformation, service design, and workflow improvement.

Software & Platform Specialisations

SAP, Salesforce, Microsoft 365, SharePoint, Dynamics, ServiceNow, configuration, administration, and functional support roles.

Testing & Quality

Manual testing, UAT, test scripts, defect management, QA reporting, and regression testing.

User Experience & Digital Content

UX/UI fundamentals, user research, prototyping, wireframes, accessibility, web content, digital comms, and tech-adjacent marketing roles.

What industries do you work with?

We partner with government agencies, tech companies, engineering firms, consultancies, startups, utilities, banks, and more. Anywhere digital, data, systems, software, infrastructure, or process improvement work is happening - our graduates thrive.
