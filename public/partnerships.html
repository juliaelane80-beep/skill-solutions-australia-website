For Host Partners

Organisational Requirements

Established ICT business or department, minimum 2 employees in technical roles, dedicated workspace and equipment, commitment to 3-month placement.

Supervision Requirements

Qualified ICT professional as supervisor, minimum 2 years industry experience, available for regular mentoring, commitment to skills development.

Project Requirements

Meaningful project work available, learning objectives clearly defined, regular feedback and evaluation, professional development opportunities.
